## Pilot-wide expansion readiness criteria

Before expanding any gate, all required pilot readiness criteria must pass with blocked items resolved:

1. No unresolved critical clinical-safety defect with pending risk treatment.
2. No unresolved critical security defect in disclosure/access/event controls.
3. Payment reconciliation evidence accepted by finance and operations.
4. Refund behavior works for pilot success/failure pathways.
5. Consultation completion quality meets approved threshold (numeric threshold to be approved under OQ-00-33).
6. Pharmacy stock accuracy and reservation recovery evidence accepted.
7. Medication delivery closure and failed-delivery recovery evidence accepted.
8. Laboratory turnaround reliability and result verification evidence accepted.
9. Critical-result acknowledgment path validated and exercised.
10. Referral follow-up completion evidence accepted.
11. Emergency escalation rehearsal evidence accepted.
12. Provider credential accuracy checks pass with periodic verification.
13. Support-case resolution within defined SLA (exact SLA is REQUIRES_APPROVAL).
14. Privacy-boundary tests prove pre-payment absence across prohibited channels.
15. Provider-detail disclosure is order-scoped and payment-scoped in tests.
16. Tenant and patient isolation tests have no cross leaks.
17. Accessibility regression in critical pilot journeys passes with approved methodology.
18. Browser-test coverage includes required failures and error paths.
19. Backup and recovery rehearsal validates reprocessing and state recovery.
20. Operations can function without direct DB edits under incident pressure.
