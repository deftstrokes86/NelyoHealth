## Resume Criteria

Every stop or pause requires root cause understood, containment completed, patient impact assessed, notifications reviewed, corrective action implemented, regression tests passed, runbook rehearsed, required approvals obtained, monitoring activated, rollback available, named authority approval, evidence retained, and communication completed.

## Pilot Review Cadence

Shift or operational, daily pilot, clinical-safety, privacy and security, finance and reconciliation, provider-operations, accessibility and design, expansion, incident-triggered, and regulatory-change reviews are review types. Final cadence is REQUIRES-OPERATIONS-APPROVAL.
