## Deletion Versus Record Integrity

Deletion requests may result in physical deletion, logical deletion, restriction, archival, de-identification, amendment, supersession, account closure, token revocation, search-index removal, cache purge, backup expiry, or legal hold. Finalized clinical records must not be silently rewritten to satisfy a correction request.

## Backup Handling

Backup handling requires legal review for deletion propagation, restore behavior, reapplication of deletion/restriction, backup access, retention, legal hold, audit, and security ownership. No backup duration is approved in P00-11.

## Expired Relationships

Employer offboarding, HMO expiry, sponsor revocation, family removal, guardian revocation, provider offboarding, or account closure ends future authority according to policy but does not delete the Patient or the patient clinical history.
