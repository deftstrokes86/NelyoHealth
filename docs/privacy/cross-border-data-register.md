## Diaspora Boundary

The sponsor may be outside Nigeria while the patient and care fulfilment remain within the approved Nigerian service area. Payment data and clinical data are separate flows. Sponsorship does not create automatic cross-border clinical disclosure. Receipts remain minimum necessary. Clinical access requires separate authorization. Medicine export and cross-border clinical practice remain outside scope.

## Transfer Risk Review

Risks requiring review: excess data, unauthorized onward transfer, weak deletion, foreign support access, unclear residency, government-access question, vendor lock-in, subprocessor change, breach notification, inability to audit, inability to export/delete, transfer of provider location, and transfer of clinical records.
