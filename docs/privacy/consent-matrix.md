## Consent Principles

- Consent is purpose-specific.
- Consent is evidenced.
- Consent is versioned.
- Consent can be withdrawn where applicable.
- Withdrawal affects future processing according to policy.
- Withdrawal does not erase valid historical audit or clinical records automatically.
- Consent is distinct from delegation.
- Consent is distinct from authorization.
- Consent is distinct from a guardian relationship.
- Consent is distinct from other possible lawful bases.
- Optional consents are separated and not preselected.
- No bundled unrelated consent or dark patterns are allowed.
