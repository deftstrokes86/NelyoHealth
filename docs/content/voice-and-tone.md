## Stable Voice

NelyoHealth voice is clear, calm, respectful, direct, human, competent, nonjudgmental, plain-language, and transparent.

## Tone Variations

| Context | Tone | Avoided words or patterns | Required reviewers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Marketing | Calm, credible, restrained | Unsupported statistics, fake testimonials, exaggerated certainty | Product + Content |
| Onboarding | Direct, step-by-step | Shame, pressure, hidden commitments | Product + Content |
| Routine clinical | Precise and non-alarming | Diagnosis promises or treatment advice without approval | Clinical + Content |
| Sensitive clinical | Respectful and careful | Casual phrasing or blame | Clinical + Content |
| Emergency | Immediate, plain, action-oriented | Decorative phrasing or uncertainty masking | Clinical + Operations |
| Financial | Transparent and specific | Ambiguous fees, wallet/coverage confusion | Finance + Legal |
| Privacy/consent | Clear boundaries and choices | Legalese-only, broad permissions | Privacy + Legal |
| Security/recovery | Direct and reassuring | Revealing sensitive data or blaming user | Security + Privacy |
| Provider operations | Efficient and precise | Patient data beyond task scope | Operations + Privacy |

## Style Rules

Use sentence-case headings unless a content owner approves otherwise. Prefer direct verbs. Avoid unexplained acronyms. Format dates, times, Nigerian currency, international currency, phone numbers, names, and honorifics consistently. Do not create final clinical, emergency, legal, or privacy wording without approval.
