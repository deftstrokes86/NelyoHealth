# NelyoHealth Brand Guide

## Mission

Make quality healthcare easier to access, coordinate and trust.

## Brand Pillars

-   Accessible
-   Coordinated
-   Trustworthy
-   Human-first
-   Inclusive
-   Secure

## Tone

Professional, warm, reassuring, practical and optimistic.

## Avoid

Marketing clichés, exaggerated claims, fear-based messaging, unnecessary
jargon.

## Key Message

Healthcare shouldn't stop after a consultation. NelyoHealth connects the
entire care journey.
