## Strategy

Content must explain, reassure, guide, prepare, confirm, warn, escalate, request action, report status, resolve error, and support decision-making without inventing claims or crossing clinical, privacy, legal, financial, or provider-disclosure boundaries.

## Content Lifecycle

Public and authenticated content must define purpose, audience, journey stage, content class, owner, review, approval, version, measurement question, retirement trigger, and supersession path. Informational content must not masquerade as transactional content; transactional content must not hide consequences.
