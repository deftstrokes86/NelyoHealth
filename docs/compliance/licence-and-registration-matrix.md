## Separation Rules

- Partner licences are not NelyoHealth licences unless a source-backed and counsel-approved legal structure says so.
- Professional credentials, facility licences, platform registrations, contract warranties, and future-scope approvals are separate evidence classes.
- NelyoHealth is not described as a PSP, bank, HMO, insurer, pharmacy, laboratory, or licensed clinical provider by this matrix.
