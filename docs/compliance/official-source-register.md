## Legal and Regulatory Disclaimer

- This document is not legal advice.
- This document does not establish that NelyoHealth is compliant, licensed, registered, accredited, approved, or authorized.
- Source findings require applicability analysis by qualified Nigerian counsel.
- Legal interpretations require qualified Nigerian counsel.
- Regulator clarification may be required.

## Source Hierarchy

1. Gazetted legislation and binding regulations.
2. Official regulator-issued rules, directives, and binding instruments.
3. Official regulator-issued guidance.
4. Official government or regulator publications and registers.
5. Official regulator webpages, portals, notices, and FAQs.
6. Court decisions or official legal materials where directly relevant.
7. Secondary materials only as research leads; no obligation is created from a secondary source alone.
