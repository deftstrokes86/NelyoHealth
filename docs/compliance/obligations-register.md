## PCN Disclosure Conflict and Launch Gate

- `REG-OBL-015` is the primary conflict row.
- The locked product rule remains unchanged: pre-payment transaction matching may expose only `providerDisplayName` plus approved non-identifying commercial/workflow fields.
- PCN Electronic Pharmacy Regulations 2026 source findings indicate platform/address/contact/licence display duties may apply to an aggregator and/or electronic-pharmaceutical service provider.
- P00-12 does not resolve whether a separate legal notice, home page, provider directory, NEPP surface, aggregator licence display, Superintendent Pharmacist contact display, or transaction quote is sufficient.
- Pharmacy matching launch is blocked until Nigerian legal counsel and likely PCN clarification confirm a compliant design.
