## Product Query Brief

Product type: healthcare and care-orchestration SaaS. Market: Nigeria. Users: patients, clinicians, provider operations, organizations, families, diaspora sponsors, finance, support, compliance, and administration. Tone: trustworthy, calm, contemporary, human, refined. Density: moderate for patients, higher for clinical and operations users. Accessibility: WCAG 2.2 AA target. Responsive: mobile-first web, tablet, desktop. Motion: subtle and purposeful.

Constraints: no generic dashboard template, excessive glassmorphism, fake statistics, unsupported health claims, decorative emergency animation, color-only status, hidden labels, or pre-payment provider-location UI.
