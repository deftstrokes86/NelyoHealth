## Brand Character

Proposed character: trustworthy, calm, contemporary, human, competent, clear, reassuring, refined, responsive, inclusive, Nigerian-market appropriate without stereotype, and premium without appearing inaccessible.

## Recommended Direction

`VIS-DIR-002 Warm Care Grid` is the proposed primary direction. It balances calm patient trust with the structure needed for clinical, pharmacy, laboratory, employer, HMO, finance, support, and administration portals. It avoids generic SaaS dashboards through a healthcare-specific state language, protected-provider patterns, patient-context indicators, clinical urgency treatment, and ledger-backed financial clarity.

Alternatives were not selected as primary because `VIS-DIR-001` is too operational for public and patient-facing surfaces, while `VIS-DIR-003` is too institutional for family, diaspora, and patient trust-building. Human design approval is still required for final palette, typography, imagery, iconography, density, and theme behavior.

## Anti-Patterns Rejected

Excessive glassmorphism, excessive gradients, decorative medical symbols everywhere, generic AI-style neon gradients, low-contrast pastel text, over-rounded controls that reduce clarity, excessive card nesting, dashboard wallpaper, emoji as structural icons, stock imagery implying unverified providers, culturally stereotyped decoration, decorative motion on emergency screens, hidden labels, color-only status communication, tiny gray text, and fake trust badges are rejected.
