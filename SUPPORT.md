# Support

## Product support

No product support is available through this repository. NelyoHealth product, patient-care, pharmacy, laboratory, payment, HMO, employer, sponsor, guardian, or clinical operations are not live in this repository.

## Development support

Use GitHub issues only for repository documentation, tooling, tests, design foundation, dependency governance, and release-readiness defects. Include synthetic or redacted evidence only.

## Security reports

Do not file public security vulnerabilities. Follow SECURITY.md and use a private repository-owner channel when private vulnerability reporting is not verified.

## Privacy concerns

Do not post real personal, patient, provider, clinical, payment, credential, organization, employer, HMO, sponsor, or guardian data. Privacy concerns require private escalation to the repository owner or designated privacy channel.

## Clinical emergencies

This repository is not an emergency-care channel and does not provide clinical advice. Emergency escalation requirements are documented for future implementation only and must never depend on repository support, payment, registration, authorization, or marketplace workflows.

## Regulatory concerns

Regulatory concerns should reference the official source, issuing body, date, and affected document. Codex may draft analysis but cannot approve legal or regulatory interpretation.

## Credentials and payment data

Do not submit real account credentials, payment credentials, API keys, tokens, browser sessions, or production URLs in issues, pull requests, logs, screenshots, traces, reports, or attachments.
